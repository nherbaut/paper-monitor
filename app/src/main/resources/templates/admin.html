My Review Setup

A paper feed is your review workspace. It groups papers, notes, workflow states, and one or more RSS feeds.

Workflow states describe where a paper is in your review process. Recommended defaults:

- Discarded
- New
- Todo
- Done

Papers are ingested from RSS feeds. The setup below creates both the paper feed and its first RSS source.
